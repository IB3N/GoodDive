About me


My name is Ben Pearce and this is my Final Project for Harvard CS50x's Introduction to Computer Science



I am a marine biologist and PADI Open Water Scuba Instructor with a passion for engaging with the world in a positive and sustainable way. I earned a masters degree from University of Southampton with experience in data and statistical analysis. I chose to immerse myself in the marine world for the past 18 months reaching almost 400 dives and 15 days dive time. While searching for a way to integrate my ocean science background and direct knowledge to benefit the marine environment, I became interested in using computer science as the link. Harvard's CS50x has proved invaluable at introducing me to the "intellectual enterprises of computer science and the art of programming". My long term goals are to continue my computer science journey, focusing on data science and analysis, while figuring out ways I can connect computer and ocean science.

Ideas for the future


Developing this project has taught me so much, and I am excited to get onto the next step. I will continue to add functionality to this site in order to keep learning:


  • - Unique background image for each site
  • - Add all the dive sites of Una-Una
  • - Page/section for all or most recent reviews
  • - Create a user profile page
  • - Let users add and display photos for each site
  • - Higher quality reviews:
    • Visibility
    • Depth
    • Dive time
    • Species seen
    • Time of dive (morning/afternoon/night)
    • Site conditions
  • - Upvote/downvote or rate reviews as helpful or not
  • - Let users add sites
  • - A more detailed sites page
  • - Google maps including sites with GPS coordinates
