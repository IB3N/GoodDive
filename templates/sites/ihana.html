The Finnish word for 'lovely' or 'wonderful'. Ihana is a gentle sloping reef with huge coral formations and is teeming with life.


Ihana is known for its large schools of yellow snapper and its resident big marbled groupers. There is a chance of seeing some blue spotted rays, turtles and schooling pick handle barracudas.
