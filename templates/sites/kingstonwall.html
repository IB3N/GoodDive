Kingston Wall is an underwater peninsula, with a vertical wall on the ocean side and a beautiful coral garden on the inside.


Looking out to the blue there is the chance of seeing large pelagic fish passing through, as well as eagle rays. The coral garden is a sheltered area for a variety of colourful reef fishes as well as cuttlefish and octopus, and rare sightings of a black-tip reef shark.
