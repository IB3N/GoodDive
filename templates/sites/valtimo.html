Valtimo is a cluster of a few habitats, each with their own charm.


The shallow fringing reef is home to reef squid, plentiful anemones with their associated fish and dancing shrimp, nudibranchs and colourful reef fish. A short swim over a sandy bottom where blue-spotted rays aggregate, brings you to a coral island dominated by soft corals and a small colony of lion fish. Nearby is a huge towering coral bommie frequented by schooling fish, as well as the odd napoleon wrasse passing by.
